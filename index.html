Where is Ha Long Bay located?

Administrative Division

  • Quang Ninh Province
  • Ha Long City
  • Parts of Hai Phong City

Regional Context

  • Northeastern Vietnam
  • Northwest Gulf of Tonkin

Geographical Location

170 km Southeast of Hanoi
In Ha Long Bay, there are many exciting activities: you can buy a ticket for a boat tour to explore the bay, go kayaking, visit stunning caves, or hike to the top of limestone mountains.
Although Ha Long Bay is beautiful and offers many exciting activities, it faces serious threats including plastic pollution, water contamination from boats and tourism, air pollution, and loss of biodiversity due to trash accumulation and habitat destruction.
In my opinion i think we should make the schedules to protect the environment and biodiversity by the activities such as raising public awareness about environmental protection and reduce waste disposal into water sources.
